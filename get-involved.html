Tax Information

AGEBETTER INITIATIVE is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (RC 8977104) and certified as tax-exempt by the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS). Nigerian taxpayers may be eligible for tax deductions on charitable contributions.

All donors receive official receipts for tax purposes. For questions about tax deductibility, please consult your tax advisor or contact our finance team at agebetter@agebetterinitiative.name.ng.
