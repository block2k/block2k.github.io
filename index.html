About Us

AYUBA FADDAH SOLID FOUNDATION is a registered nonprofit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged children in Nigeria through education.

We believe that education is a fundamental right that should be accessible to all children, regardless of their economic circumstances. Our foundation works tirelessly to bridge the gap between poverty and educational opportunity.

Our Mission & Programs

Our Mission

Our goal is to raise funds to provide classroom spaces, educational materials, and school enrollment support for children who are currently excluded from formal education due to poverty. Every donation helps us create learning opportunities - from community classroom funding to supporting school supplies for underprivileged pupils. Your contribution will directly support the AYUBA FADDAH SOLID FOUNDATION children education relief fund program for children in Nigeria.

Classroom Construction & Community Spaces

Educational Materials & School Supplies

School Enrollment Support
