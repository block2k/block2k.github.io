Who We Are

About THE ROCK FOUNDATION SCHOOLS OBUDU LTD

We are a nonprofit educational foundation based in Obudu, Cross River State, Nigeria. Our mission is to remove financial barriers that prevent children from attending school. We work with families, schools, and community leaders to make education accessible, safe, and consistent for every child.

Our Mission

Education should be a right, not a privilege.

Many children in Nigeria are at risk of dropping out of school because their families cannot afford school fees, uniforms, or basic learning materials. At THE ROCK FOUNDATION SCHOOLS OBUDU LTD, we believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and pursue their dreams – regardless of their economic background.

By partnering with local schools and communities, we identify children in urgent need and provide targeted support that keeps them in the classroom and gives them hope for a better future.

  • Paying school fees for children from low-income households
  • Providing uniforms, books, and essential learning materials
  • Supporting community-based learning spaces and after-school programs
  • Engaging parents and guardians on the value of continuous education

What We Do

Our Core Programs

Our work centers on practical, direct support that keeps children in school and allows them to learn with dignity.

Program 01

School Fees Support

We provide partial or full payment of school fees for children whose families are unable to meet termly or yearly educational costs.

  • Identifying vulnerable children in partnership with schools
  • Covering tuition and compulsory fees
  • Priority for children at risk of dropping out
Program 02

Uniforms & Learning Materials

Many children attend school without proper uniforms or learning tools. We step in to restore dignity and help them learn effectively.

  • School uniforms, sandals, and basic clothing
  • Exercise books, textbooks, writing materials
  • Simple school bags and supplies
Program 03

Community Learning Support

Beyond fees, we support community-driven initiatives that create safe, inclusive learning environments for children in Obudu.

  • Support for community classroom spaces
  • After-school tutoring and homework assistance
  • Education awareness for parents and guardians
