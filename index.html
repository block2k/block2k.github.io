Our Mission

Breaking the cycle of poverty through community-led solutions

AGEBETTER INITIATIVE partners with Nigerian communities to design and implement sustainable programs that address the root causes of poverty. We focus on four key pillars: nutrition security, quality education, accessible healthcare, and women's economic empowerment.
