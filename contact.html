Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to AGEBETTER INITIATIVE?

You can donate via bank transfer to our account (details on the Get Involved page) or contact us to set up monthly giving. All donors receive official tax receipts.

Are donations tax-deductible in Nigeria?

Yes. AGEBETTER INITIATIVE is registered with CAC and certified as tax-exempt by FIRS. Nigerian taxpayers may be eligible for tax deductions. Please consult your tax advisor for specific guidance.

How can I apply for program support?

If you or someone you know needs assistance from our programs (nutrition, education, healthcare, or economic empowerment), please email agebetter@agebetterinitiative.name.ng. Our team will guide you through the eligibility and application process.

Do you accept international volunteers?

Yes, we welcome skilled professionals from around the world, especially those with expertise in healthcare, education, technology, and business development. However, volunteers must arrange their own travel, accommodation, and work permits. Contact agebetter@agebetterinitiative.name.ng for details.

Can I visit your programs in person?

Donors, partners, and volunteers are welcome to schedule program site visits. Please contact us at least two weeks in advance to arrange visits. We prioritize beneficiary privacy and safety in all site visits.

How do you ensure accountability and transparency?

We publish annual reports with independently audited financial statements, maintain detailed program monitoring systems, and provide regular updates to donors. Our Board of Trustees provides governance oversight, and we adhere to international nonprofit best practices.

Can my company partner with UbuntuCare?

Absolutely! We offer various corporate partnership opportunities including program sponsorships, employee giving programs, skills-based volunteering, and in-kind donations. Contact agebetter@agebetterinitiative.name.ng to discuss options that align with your CSR goals.

How can I receive updates about your work?

You can subscribe to our quarterly newsletter by emailing agebetter@agebetterinitiative.name.ng with "Subscribe to Newsletter" in the subject line.
