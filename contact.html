Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our work and how you can help

How can I donate to the foundation?

You can support us by reaching out through our contact form above, selecting "Making a Donation." We'll provide you with secure donation options including bank transfer details and online payment methods.

Are donations tax-deductible?

As a registered non-profit organization, donations made to Ocheikwu Empowerment Foundation may be tax-deductible in accordance with Nigerian tax laws. We provide official receipts for all donations for tax purposes.

Where do my donations go?

100% of donations directly support our programs - educational scholarships, vocational training, skill development workshops, and humanitarian aid. We maintain complete transparency with quarterly reports showing exactly how funds are utilized.

Can I volunteer with OEF?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers who want to share their time, skills, and expertise. Whether you can help with training, mentoring, or program support, reach out through our contact form.

How can my organization partner with OEF?

We're always looking for strategic partnerships with organizations that share our vision. Contact us to explore collaboration opportunities including program sponsorship, employee volunteering, skills transfer, and joint initiatives.

Who can benefit from your programs?

Our programs serve underprivileged individuals and communities who lack access to education, skills training, and economic opportunities. Selection is based on need assessment and program criteria. If you know someone who could benefit, please reach out to us.

How do I stay updated on OEF's work?

Follow us on social media and subscribe to our updates by sending us your email through the contact form. We regularly share success stories, program updates, and quarterly impact reports.

Is my information secure?

Yes. We take data privacy seriously and comply with all data protection regulations. Your personal information is kept confidential and used only for communication about our programs and your donation/involvement.
